As I din’t had much exposure to blog and blog generators, I started with Google’s blogger and blogged about my achievements on a montly basis. Once I gained some confidence on blogging and having decided to blog on a regular basis, I purchased a domain. Then I got introduced to the concept of static site generators and github-pages in the form of Octopress when my mentor had migrated to it.
